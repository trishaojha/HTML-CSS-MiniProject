Who Is Aniyia Williams?

Aniyia Williams is a tech entrepreneur, activist, and the founder of Black & Brown Founders and Tinsel. She's dedicated to creating opportunities for underrepresented entrepreneurs in tech and building tools that blend creativity with innovation.

Main Accomplishments

  • Founded Black & Brown Founders to help BIPOC entrepreneurs launch tech businesses.
  • Created Tinsel, a wearable tech company merging fashion and electronics.
  • Co-founded Zebras Unite, a global movement for ethical, community-centered startups.
